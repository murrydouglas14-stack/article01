**How does QuickBooks Desktop Do Multi-User work?**
---------------------------------------------------

QuickBooks Desktop enables the multiple-user mode for two or more users to access the same file or data at the same time. It is useful on particular time for businesses with accounting teams, managers, or bookkeepers who want real-time access to financial data. 

When setting up multi-user in QuickBooks Desktop, we recommend keeping your business data on your local computer server in the hard drive. This makes it easier to share them over your network.
