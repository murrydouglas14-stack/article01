Step-by-Step Method to Call QuickBooks Online Payroll Support 
--------------------------------------------------------------

Step 1 - First, you log in to your QuickBooks Online account in official site.

Step 2 - After you can click the Help icon in the upper right corner.

Step 3 - Then you describe your payroll issue in the search bar and hit Enter.

Step 4 - If your issues are unresolved, then you select contact us at the bottom.

Step 5 - After you choose payroll as the topic, explain your issue, and select talk to a specialist or call us.

Step 6 - You can give your account details if prompted. This qualifies you for support and displays your specific QuickBooks Online Payroll Support Phone Number, hours, and callback option.
